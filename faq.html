Frequently Asked Questions

Back to Campus Hub
1. How do I sign up for the EV Commute service? +

Signing up is quick! Click on the "Sign up" button in the header of the main page and follow the simple steps. You'll need your campus email and student ID for verification to unlock student pricing.

2. Can I use the service and website on my mobile device? +

Absolutely! Our website is fully **responsive** and works seamlessly on all devices, including your phone and tablet. Our dedicated **mobile app** (coming soon) will provide an even smoother experience for real-time bookings and charger location.

3. Where can I find the prices and available student discounts for the vehicles? +

Prices and full vehicle specifications are listed on the main page under the **"Zero Emissions - Campus fleets"** section. Scroll down, choose a vehicle type (Scooter, Bike, or Cycle), and click on a brand. You will see the estimated on-road price and any **student-exclusive offers** (like 0% EMI or service credits) in the details panel.

4. What kind of electric vehicles are available for campus commute? +

We offer three main categories: Scooters (e.g., Ola S1 Pro, Ather 450X), Bikes (e.g., Revolt RV400, Ultraviolette F77), and Cycles (e.g., Yulu Miracle). Each is suitable for different commute needs and ranges.

5. How do the Smart Charging Stations work? +

Our smart chargers efficiently allocate power, often scheduling charging during low-tariff hours to save costs. You can use our **map integration** (coming soon) to find available spots and use your **student credits** for frictionless charging.
